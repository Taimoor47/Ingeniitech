About Us

InGenii-Tech platform brings together a conglomerate of in field Information Technology Professional, Academicians and Managers with an objective of providing training to qualified participants in high demand digital career.

An education tech company that provides a path to skilled careers for adults with or without a bachelor’s Degrees. Our programs are fast, flexible, and focused on market and employers needs.

Mission

To provide an exceptional online learning experience that enhances the lives and careers.

Goal

To select, guide and prepare program participants for high-growth careers in the Digital Economy to bridge the gap between employers’ needs and participants’ skills.

Approach

Innovative Agile Curriculum with flexible formats where students learn the same modules, take part in exercises, and gain skills employers need.

In-Demand

At Ingenii-Tech, With a focus on High-Demand and emerging fields with an upward mobility within the information technology domain Partnering with major employers and use labor market data to understand the specific competencies required for skilled roles.

Supported learning

We teach the skills employers need through “blended” learning programs, which combine the best of online learning with live support. Our program includes weekly small group meetings, dedicated coaching, as well as community-wide events.

Talented Adults

We recruit working adults with demonstrated potential to succeed in our program and the careers that follow. Our participants don’t have the time or money to participate in bootcamps or go back to school, and we providea flexible pathway for quickly advancing their careers.

This CompTIA Security+ training course provides the foundational knowledge you need to pass the CompTIA Security+ SY0-601 certification exam, and the skills to ensure compliance and operational security in your organization.

CompTIA Security+ certification is a vendor-neutral certification that is recognized worldwide as a benchmark for information system security best practices. Earning Security+ certification demonstrates your knowledge of industry-wide information assurance topics, like systems and network security, network infrastructure, access control, assessments and audits, cryptography, and organizational security.

Data Analysis

Learn the basics of Data Anlaysis

Data analysis and analytics are evolving disciplines. We constantly hear about big data, prediction, AI, and modeling techniques.

However, advanced techniques rest on fundamentals which can be applied in many job roles. This class quickly equips you with that foundation. Whether you're charting your overall business intelligence strategy or performing analysis yourself, these basic tools and techniques rapidly inform effective decision-making. In this fast-paced introductory workshop, we'll examine the history of business intelligence, its relationship to data analysis, and why the two are needed to help businesses deliver a complete assembly of the 'data puzzle'. We'll also address hurdles teams face when dealing with data overload and suggests some possible solutions.

Amid an ongoing explosion of data, there's also a greater need to understand who is qualified to correctly analyze data. We will explore the qualifications of data analysts as well as the analytic tools available for those people to use associated with the position.

IT HELP DESK FOR BEGINNERS

ABOUT DESKTOP SUPPORT SPECIALIST

A desktop support specialist is an information technology (IT) professional who maintains computer hardware and software systems. They're responsible for installing new programs, managing updates and providing technical support. A Desktop Support Specialist is generally hired by a company to oversee and maintain their computer hardware and software systems. Their skills assist the company in resolving technical issues concerning customer's accounts or company software infrastructure.
